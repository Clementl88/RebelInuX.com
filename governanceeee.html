Cultural Governance: RebelJudges

RebelJudges Voting Key

"RebelJudges Voting Key" – Governance NFT for community-led meme contest judging. 1 Voting Key = 1 Vote with $REBL reward incentives.

Key Details

  • Token Name: RebelJudges Voting Key
  • Token Details: 1,000,000,000 supply on ZORA (Base)
  • Governance Power: 1 Voting Key = 1 Vote in all meme contests
  • Voting Rights: Holders decide winners of community meme contests
