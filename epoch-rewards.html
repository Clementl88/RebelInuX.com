The Epoch Reward Formula

Understand how rewards are calculated and simulate your potential earnings

Community-Approved Formula: This system was proposed, debated, and approved by $REBL token holders through our decentralized governance process.

Formula Components

Rewardᵢ = (WSᵢ / ∑WS) × WERP × γ
WSᵢ (Your Weighted Share)

WSᵢ = Σ[Token Batch × (1 + 0.07 × min(Epoch Age, 20))]

Your loyalty metric: 7% weight boost per epoch, up to 2.4x at Epoch 20.

∑WS (Total Active Weighted Shares)

Sum of weighted shares from ALL CURRENT PARTICIPANTS in that epoch.

Focuses rewards on the active community, not idle holders.

WERP (Weekly Reward Pool)

3.2M $REBL per epoch

Fixed weekly distribution from the treasury allocation.

γ (Gamma Scale Factor)

γ = MAX(0.4, MIN(1, P/(0.5 × CS), CS × 1.5/∑WS))

Our sustainability engine with three layers of protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are epochs?

Epochs are 7-day periods during which you can claim $REBL rewards. Each epoch uses a specific Rebel Key NFT for claims.

Do I need both assets?

Yes, you need both Creator Coin ($rebelinux) and the active Rebel Key NFT to claim rewards.

How are rewards calculated exactly?

Using our epoch reward formula: Rewardᵢ = (WSᵢ / ∑WS) × WERP × γ. See detailed formula explanations →

How do I estimate my rewards?

Use our Reward Calculator for personalized estimates based on your token holdings and age.

When are rewards distributed?

$REBL tokens are distributed to your Solana wallet within 48 hours of the epoch ending.

What is the gamma factor (γ)?

A sustainability engine that scales rewards based on participation (40-100% of pool), protecting the treasury.

What happens if I miss an epoch?

You can claim in the next epoch. Your token age continues to accumulate, potentially increasing future rewards.
