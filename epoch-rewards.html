The Oblivion Flame Mechanism

The rebelinux_burn account holds $rebelinux tokens, which qualify it for a share of every epoch's $REBL reward pool if it submits its Solana address to the active Rebel Key within the epoch window, just like any other participant. The $REBL tokens it earns are permanently burned (sent to an unspendable address), creating deflationary pressure. The original $rebelinux in the account are not burned and continue to be eligible for future epochs.

This is the "CS" used in your rewards: Every epoch reward is calculated using this exact 499,242,047 $rebelinux as the base supply.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are epochs?

Epochs are 7-day periods during which you can claim $REBL rewards. Each epoch uses a specific Rebel Key NFT for claims.

Do I need both assets?

Yes, you need both Creator Coin ($rebelinux) and the active Rebel Key NFT to claim rewards.

How are rewards calculated?

Rewards are proportional to your weighted share of the total Creator Coin supply. Older tokens earn more (age bonus up to 20 epochs).

When are rewards distributed?

$REBL tokens are distributed to your Solana wallet within 48 hours of the epoch ending.

Can I sell my Creator Coin?

Yes, but the system uses LIFO (Last-In-First-Out). Newest tokens are sold first, preserving age of older holdings.

What happens if I miss an epoch?

You can claim in the next epoch. Your token age continues to accumulate, potentially increasing future rewards.
