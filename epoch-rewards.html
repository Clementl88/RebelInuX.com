Frequently Asked Questions

What are epochs?

Epochs are 7-day periods during which you can claim $REBL rewards. Each epoch uses a specific Rebel Key NFT for claims.

Do I need both assets?

Yes, you need both Creator Coin ($rebelinux) and the active Rebel Key NFT to claim rewards.

How are rewards calculated exactly?

Using our sustainable formula: Rewardᵢ = (WSᵢ / ∑WS) × 3.2M × γ. See detailed formula explanations →

How do I estimate my rewards?

Use our Reward Calculator for personalized estimates based on your token holdings and age.

When are rewards distributed?

$REBL tokens are distributed to your Solana wallet within 48 hours of the epoch ending.

What is the gamma factor (γ)?

A sustainability engine that scales rewards based on participation (40-100% of pool), protecting the treasury.

What happens if I miss an epoch?

You can claim in the next epoch. Your token age continues to accumulate, potentially increasing future rewards.
