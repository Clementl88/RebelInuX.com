COMMUNITY GOVERNANCE

At RebelInuX, we believe in decentralized decision-making. Our community governance model ensures that every holder has a voice in the project's future direction.

Proposal System

Submit ideas and improvements for the project. Community votes determine which proposals get implemented.

Token Weighted Voting

Voting power proportional to $REBL holdings. Every token counts in decision-making.

Community Treasury

Ecosystem funds managed collectively for marketing, development, and community initiatives.

Governance Process

1

Proposal Submission

Any community member can submit proposals through our governance portal

2

Community Discussion

Proposals are discussed in detail across all community channels

3

Formal Voting

Token holders vote on proposals using their $REBL tokens

4

Implementation

Approved proposals are implemented by the development team
