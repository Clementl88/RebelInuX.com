COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Our Community Values

To maintain a positive and productive environment for all rebels, we ask everyone to follow these simple guidelines.

Respect Everyone

Treat all community members with respect, regardless of experience level or background.

No Spam or Scams

Keep discussions constructive. No spam, scam links, or shilling other projects.

Help New Rebels

Welcome newcomers and help them understand the ecosystem. We grow together.

Constructive Criticism

Feedback is welcome, but keep it constructive and helpful to the community.

Stay Positive

Keep discussions positive. Unsubstantiated fear and toxicity hurt community growth.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to RebelInuX and the broader crypto ecosystem.

Violation of guidelines may result in removal from community channels.
