Community Guidelines

Our Community Values

  • Respect Everyone: Treat all community members with respect, regardless of experience level or background.
  • No Spam or Scams: Keep discussions constructive. No spam, scam links, or shilling other projects.
  • Help New Rebels: Welcome newcomers and help them understand the ecosystem. We grow together.
  • Constructive Criticism: Feedback is welcome, but keep it constructive and helpful to the community.
  • No FUD or Toxicity: Keep discussions positive. Unsubstantiated fear and toxicity hurt community growth.
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to RebelInuX and the broader crypto ecosystem.

Violation of guidelines may result in removal from community channels.
