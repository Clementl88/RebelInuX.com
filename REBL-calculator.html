Calculator FAQ

What is the Gamma Scale Factor (γ)?

γ adjusts rewards based on participation: 40% minimum, scaling to 100% when 50%+ of supply participates. γ = MAX(0.4, MIN(1, P/(0.5×CS), CS/∑WS))

How is WSᵢ calculated?

WSᵢ = Token Amount × (1 + 0.07 × min(Epoch Age, 20)). Each batch gets a 7% weight boost per epoch, maxing at 2.4x after 20 epochs.

Why do my estimates change with participation?

The formula is proportional: Your share = Your WSᵢ ÷ Total WS. More participants = your share changes even if your tokens stay the same.

What's the difference between P and ∑WS?

P = Total participating tokens (raw amount). ∑WS = Total weighted shares (includes age bonuses). ∑WS is always ≥ P.

Is this calculator accurate?

This uses the exact formula from smart contracts. Results are estimates based on current participation levels; actual rewards depend on final epoch data.

How often should I recalculate?

Recalculate when: 1) You buy/sell tokens, 2) An epoch completes (age increases), 3) Participation changes significantly.
