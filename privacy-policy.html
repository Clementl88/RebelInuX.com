Privacy FAQ

Do you sell my data?

No. Never. We don't collect personal data, so there's nothing to sell. We do not and will never sell user information.

What about my wallet address?

Wallet addresses are public on the Solana blockchain. RebelInuX does not track, store, or link wallet addresses to identities. Blockchain explorers like Solscan show public data.

Do you use Google Analytics?

No. We use privacy-focused, anonymized analytics that do not track individuals or store personal data. No information is shared with advertising platforms.

How do I opt out of cookies?

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they're required for site functionality. Optional analytics cookies can be blocked via browser settings.

Is my email stored?

If you email us, your email is stored in our email system only to respond. We never add emails to lists, share them, or use them for marketing.

Do you comply with GDPR?

Yes. Our zero-data-collection approach inherently complies with GDPR's data minimization principles. EEA users have full rights despite no data being stored.
