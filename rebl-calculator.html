Calculator FAQ

What is the Collector Multiplier?

The Collector Multiplier rewards holders of Journey Collectibles: +0.05x per collectible and +0.1x if you're the largest holder of a collectible. It multiplies your entire weighted share (WSᵢ).

Multiplierᵢ = 1.0 + 0.05×Collectibles + 0.1×LargestHolder

How do I account for other participants?

Use the "Other Participants" section to add their token batches with ages, or use summary mode to set total tokens and average age.

Detailed batches give most accurate results

What if I don't know other participants' details?

Use the "Summary" mode to estimate total other tokens and average age. For realistic estimates, use 100-200M tokens with 5-10 epoch average age.

Example: 150M tokens with 8 epoch average age

How does age affect rewards?

Each epoch (week) adds +7% weight bonus, maxing at +140% after 20 epochs. Older tokens get more weight in the WS calculation.

Age bonus encourages long-term holding

What's the Gamma Scale Factor (γ)?

γ adjusts rewards based on participation: 40% minimum, scaling to 100% when 50%+ of supply participates. γ = MAX(0.4, MIN(1, P/(0.5×CS), CS×1.5/∑WS))

Protects treasury while rewarding active participation

Is this calculator accurate?

This uses the exact formula from the whitepaper. Accuracy depends on how well you estimate other participants' tokens and ages.

Provides realistic estimates based on your inputs

How often should I recalculate?

Recalculate when: 1) You buy/sell tokens, 2) An epoch completes (age increases), 3) You learn about other participants' holdings, 4) Your collectible holdings change.

Keep your inputs updated for accurate estimates

How do I actually claim rewards?

You need all three assets: $rebelinux Creator Coin, the Permanent Rebel Key, and the most recent Journey Collectible. See step-by-step claiming instructions →

Weekly claiming through the official interface
