Frequently Asked Questions

What is the total supply of $REBL?

The total supply is fixed at 1,000,000,000 (1 billion) $REBL tokens, with a deflationary mechanism through buyback and burn.

How are ZORA holders rewarded?

160M $REBL (16% of total supply) is allocated to reward $rebelinux Creator Coin holders through weekly epoch distributions.

What is the Founder's Commitment?

The founder has locked 161.84M $REBL (16.23% of supply) for 1 year with no cliff, demonstrating long-term commitment.

How does the buyback & burn work?

80% of ZORA revenue is used to buy back and permanently burn $REBL tokens, creating deflationary pressure.

What is the Community DAO Reserve?

10M $REBL (1% of supply) is reserved for community governance initiatives, locked until July 2026.

How can I verify vesting contracts?

All vesting contracts are publicly verifiable on Streamflow. Links are provided for each allocation.
