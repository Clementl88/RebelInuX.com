Key Tokenomics Features

The Ecosystem Fund (60M REBL, 6% of total supply) is designated for development, audits, marketing, and operational costs. It is divided into two portions:

  • Initial Operations (0.5%): 5,000,000 REBL - Fully allocated at TGE for essential costs during first 6 months
  • Sustained Growth (5.5%): 55,000,000 REBL - Subject to 6-month cliff, then 20% unlocked followed by linear vesting over 18 months

On-Chain Verification

Ecosystem Vesting Contract Ecosystem Multi-Sig Wallet

The ZORA Rewards Treasury (160M REBL) is strategically split into two components:

  • Asset Holder Rewards (80% | 128M REBL): Dedicated to rewarding holders of Creator Coins and Content Coin NFTs
  • Community Incentives Pool (20% | 32M REBL): For grassroots community initiatives, meme contests, and creator giveaways

Each RebelInuX Creator Coin represents a foundational claim on future REBL tokens from the Solana blockchain, drawn directly from the ZORA Rewards Treasury.

Vesting Schedule

The entire treasury is subject to transparent vesting:

  • 40% (64M REBL) Cliff Release: Released immediately for initial claims
  • 60% (96M REBL) Linear Vesting: Distributed over 2 years (concluding September 14, 2027)

The Community DAO Reserve (10M REBL, 1% of total supply) is the treasury fund for the RebelInuX DAO, dedicated to its long-term health and growth.

Vesting Schedule: Cliff until July 1, 2026, then 20% (2M REBL) unlocked, followed by linear vesting of the remaining 80% (8M REBL) over 24 months.

On-Chain Verification

Vesting Contract Multisig Recipient

Upon unlock, control will be transferred to the RebelInuX DAO for community-governed initiatives:

  • Strategic Partnerships & Collaborations
  • Community Grants & Initiatives
  • Exchange Listings
  • Ecosystem Incentives
  • Security & Audits

RebelInuX leverages a multi-chain strategy to maximize value for REBL holders:

  • Revenue Generation on ZORA: Trading fees from Creator Coin and Content Coin NFTs generate ETH income
  • Strategic Allocation: 80% bridged to Solana to buy back and burn REBL, 15% to Community DAO Reserve, 5% for operational costs
  • Value Distribution: Purchased REBL tokens are burned, increasing scarcity and value

This creates a powerful cross-chain feedback loop where ZORA asset success directly translates into buying pressure and value accumulation for the REBL token on Solana.

In our commitment to transparency, the project has renounced Freeze Authority, Mint Authority, and Update Authority. This means:

  • No central authority can freeze or mint new tokens
  • All updates are community-driven
  • Ensuring a fair and trustless environment for our rebels

Built on Solana's secure platform, our rebellion is fully decentralized and unstoppable.
