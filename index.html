RebelInuX leverages a multi-chain strategy to maximize value for REBL holders:

  • Revenue Generation on ZORA: Trading fees from Creator Coin and Content Coin NFTs generate ETH income
  • Strategic Allocation: 80% bridged to Solana to buy back and burn REBL, 15% to Community DAO Reserve, 5% for operational costs
  • Value Distribution: Purchased REBL tokens are burned, increasing scarcity and value

This creates a powerful cross-chain feedback loop where ZORA asset success directly translates into buying pressure and value accumulation for the REBL token on Solana.

At RebelInuX, our foundational principles are transparency, decentralization, and security. We believe that true empowerment arises when control is distributed across the community.

$REBL Token on Solana:

  • Contract Renouncement: The Freeze Authority, Mint Authority, and Update Authority for the $REBL token smart contract have been permanently renounced.
  • No Rug Pulls: No single entity can freeze token transfers or mint new $REBL tokens.
  • Immutable Code: The smart contract code is now immutable.
  • Liquidity Protection: The initial REBL/SOL pool will have 90% of its LP tokens locked for one year at launch using UNCX Network.

Creator Coins & Content Coin NFTs on ZORA:

  • Founder Commitment: 50% of the Creator Coins supply is allocated to the founder and subject to a 5-year linear vesting schedule.
  • Decentralized Ownership: Each asset is owned directly by the holder, secured by the ZORA protocol on Base.
  • Immutable Contracts: Smart contracts are non-upgradeable.
  • Censorship-Resistant: Assets cannot be altered, frozen, or seized by any central party.

The tokenomics outlined represent the foundational layer of the RebelInuX ecosystem. Our commitment is to evolve these mechanics transparently under community guidance.
