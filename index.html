Our Mascot Travels Through Time

The RebelInuX mascot is doing time and space travel to assist historical events aligned with its rebellious nature, painting the scenes as they unfold. We use real historical paintings and animate them with AI, transforming static art into living, breathing video collectibles.

Currently, the journey is witnessing the 1789 French Revolution, a defining moment of rebellion and change. But this is just the beginning. Future journeys will explore historical events from all countries and cultures.

This is a collective work. You decide our next destinations, which historical moments to capture, which scenes are important, and what elements to add, including what our mascot wears in each era. Participate in the Governance Hub to shape history.
