Decentralization & Security

In our commitment to transparency and decentralization, the project has renounced the Freeze Authority, Mint Authority, and Update Authority. This means:

  • No central authority can freeze or mint new tokens
  • All updates are community-driven
  • Ensuring a fair and trustless environment for our rebels

Built on Solana's secure platform, our rebellion is fully decentralized and unstoppable.

Future Burning Mechanism

We are committed to establishing a transparent and systematic token burning process in the future. This mechanism will involve periodically reducing the circulating supply by burning a percentage of tokens from designated wallets. Details such as the frequency and percentage of tokens to be burned will be communicated transparently through updates on our website and in the white paper. Stay tuned as we work towards making our ecosystem more deflationary and community-driven.
