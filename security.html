Security Best Practices

How to Stay Safe

  • Always verify the contract address - Compare with the official address shown above
  • Never share your private keys or seed phrase - No legitimate service will ever ask for these
  • Bookmark official websites - Use bookmarks instead of searching to avoid phishing sites
  • Verify social media accounts - Check for blue verification badges on official accounts
  • Use hardware wallets - For significant holdings, consider using a Ledger or Trezor
  • Start with small test transactions - When interacting with new contracts, test with small amounts first
    • Remember

    If something seems too good to be true, it probably is. Always do your own research (DYOR) and verify information from multiple sources.
