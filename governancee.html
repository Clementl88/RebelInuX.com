Decentralized Governance System

RebelInuX operates on a true DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) model. Every $REBL token represents a voting share, giving holders direct influence over the project's development, treasury management, and future direction.

Governance Principles

One Token, One Vote

Each $REBL token equals one vote. No special privileges or weighted voting.

Complete Transparency

All proposals, votes, and treasury transactions are publicly visible on-chain.

Secure Voting

Voting occurs on-chain with cryptographic verification. No manipulation possible.

Community-Driven

Any $REBL holder can submit proposals. No gatekeeping or approval committees.

Fair Timeframes

Standard 7-day voting periods ensure everyone has time to review and vote.

Incentivized Participation

Active governance participants receive NFT rewards and special recognition.

Voting Quorum & Rules

For a proposal to pass, it must meet the following criteria:

  • 40% Quorum: Minimum 40% of circulating $REBL must participate in vote
  • 51% Majority: Simple majority of votes must be in favor
  • 7-Day Period: Standard voting duration for all proposals
  • One Vote Per Token: Each $REBL token equals one vote
  • No Delegation: Direct voting only - no vote delegation system

Governance Guide

How to Vote

  1. Connect your wallet containing $REBL tokens
  2. Navigate to "Active Votes" section
  3. Review proposal details thoroughly
  4. Select your vote (For, Against, or Abstain)
  5. Confirm transaction in your wallet
  6. Your vote is recorded on-chain immediately

Writing Effective Proposals

  • Be Specific: Clearly define what you want to achieve
  • Provide Context: Explain why this proposal is needed
  • Include Details: Timeline, budget, and implementation plan
  • Consider Risks: Address potential downsides
  • Get Feedback: Discuss in Telegram before submitting
  • Be Realistic: Propose achievable goals

Treasury Management

  • Community treasury holds 20% of total supply
  • Funds are allocated via successful proposals only
  • Multi-signature wallet with 3/5 signatures required
  • Monthly transparency reports published
  • Emergency fund: 5% of treasury for urgent needs
  • All transactions publicly verifiable on-chain

Governance FAQ

What's the minimum $REBL to submit a proposal?

10,000 $REBL tokens are required to submit a governance proposal.

How long do votes last?

All proposals have a standard 7-day voting period.

Can I change my vote?

No, votes are final and cannot be changed once submitted.

What happens if quorum isn't met?

Proposals fail automatically if less than 40% of circulating supply votes.

Are there voting rewards?

Yes, active voters receive NFT rewards and special community recognition.

Can the team veto proposals?

No, the team has no veto power. All decisions are made by token holders.
