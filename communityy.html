Community Guidelines

✅ DOs

  • Be respectful - Treat all community members with kindness and respect
  • Help others - Share knowledge and assist new members
  • Stay on topic - Keep discussions relevant to RebelInuX and crypto
  • Share responsibly - Provide accurate information and cite sources
  • Report issues - Help moderators maintain a safe environment

❌ DON'Ts

  • No harassment - Absolutely no bullying, hate speech, or discrimination
  • No spam - Avoid excessive posting, promotion, or off-topic content
  • No financial advice - Never tell others what to do with their money
  • No scams - Zero tolerance for fraudulent activities or phishing
  • No FUD - Avoid spreading fear, uncertainty, and doubt without evidence

Important: Violation of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary mutes, or permanent bans. We're committed to maintaining a positive, safe, and productive community for all rebels.
