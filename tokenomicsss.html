The "Oblivion Flame" Mechanism
The rebelinux_burn account holds $rebelinux tokens, which qualify it for a share of every epoch's $REBL reward pool. The $REBL tokens it earns are permanently burned, creating deflationary pressure while the original $rebelinux continue to be eligible for future epochs.
