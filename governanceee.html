Governance FAQ

What is the difference between RebelJudges and the DAO?

RebelJudges handles cultural governance (meme contests, community events) while the DAO will handle financial governance (treasury, partnerships, protocol decisions). This separation ensures specialized governance for different aspects of our ecosystem.

When will the DAO launch?

The RebelInuX DAO is planned for launch in 2026. The exact timeline will be determined through community discussions and voting. This gradual approach ensures proper testing and community preparation.

How do I earn rewards from RebelJudges voting?

When you vote in meme contests using RebelJudges Voting Keys, and your selected entries win, you earn proportional $REBL rewards. This creates a self-sustaining ecosystem where governance participation is directly rewarded.

What will the DAO control?

The DAO will control: Treasury management, Partnership approvals, Protocol upgrades, Grant distributions, Exchange listing decisions, and other strategic financial decisions affecting the RebelInuX ecosystem.

Can I participate in governance without buying NFTs?

Yes! While RebelJudges requires Voting Key NFTs, the future DAO will use $REBL tokens for voting. You can participate in DAO governance by holding $REBL tokens. Additionally, everyone can participate in community discussions regardless of holdings.

How many $REBL tokens do I need for governance?

There's no minimum requirement for basic voting in the future DAO (1 $REBL = 1 vote). However, submitting proposals may require a minimum token amount, which will be determined through community governance.
