Key Whitepaper v2.0 Information

Triple-Asset NFTfi Ecosystem

$rebelinux Creator Coin + Permanent Rebel Key + Journey NFTs on ZORA (Base) work together to distribute $REBL rewards on Solana.

Age-Weighted Rewards

+7% per epoch per batch. At 20 epochs, a one-time +20% loyalty bonus is applied to that batch only.

80% Buy-Back & Burn

80% of ZORA creator fees buy back and burn $REBL, creating continuous deflationary pressure.

REBL Governance Hub

Token-gated Telegram via Collab.land. 500K $REBL threshold, 1 member = 1 vote. Controls Journey Contest winners and protocol parameters.

NFTfi Epoch Contests

Competitive events spanning multiple epochs with rewards in $REBL, $rebelinux, SOL, stablecoins, and other cryptos.

Journey Contest

Community members vote on next historical theme (60% vote + 40% engagement). Winner receives public credit, crypto rewards, $rebelinux, and free mint.
