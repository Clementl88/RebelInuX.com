Artwork & NFT FAQ

How can I submit my artwork?

Share your artwork on Telegram, Twitter/X, or Discord using the links above. Include your social handle and wallet address for credit and prizes.

What are the contest prizes?

Weekly meme contests offer $REBL token prizes: 1st place 500K $REBL, 2nd place 250K $REBL, 3rd place 100K $REBL.

Where can I buy/sell NFTs?

On Magic Eden, Tensor, and other Solana NFT marketplaces. Also check ZORA for community artwork collections.

Can I submit community art?

Yes! Use the submission channels above to share your RebelInuX creations for a chance to be featured.

What are NFT utilities?

Governance power, airdrops, staking rewards, and exclusive access to events and future collections.

How do I mint NFTs?

Connect wallet, upload artwork, set details, and pay the minting fee. Use our platform or ZORA for easy minting.
