Detailed Tokenomics Breakdown

The Team Fund (70M REBL, 7%) is dedicated to compensating and retaining core contributors, with a transparent vesting structure for optimal long-term alignment.

  • Immediate Operations (40%): 28M REBL available for strategic initiatives and essential costs.
  • Long-Term Alignment (60%): 42M REBL secured in 3-year linear vesting with 40% initial cliff.
Vesting Contract Multi-Sig Wallet

The Ecosystem Fund (60M REBL, 6%) is allocated for development, audits, and marketing, with a 6-month cliff and 18-month linear vesting.

  • Initial Operations (0.5%): 5M REBL for essential costs during the first 6 months.
  • Sustained Growth (5.5%): 55M REBL, subject to a 6-month cliff, then 20% unlocked followed by linear vesting over 18 months.
Vesting Contract Multi-Sig Wallet

The ZORA Rewards Treasury (160M REBL, 16%) is split into:

  • Asset Holder Rewards (80%): 128M REBL for Creator Coin and Content Coin NFT holders.
  • Community Incentives (20%): 32M REBL for grassroots initiatives and contests.

Vesting: 40% (64M REBL) released at launch, 60% (96M REBL) linearly over 2 years.

View Vesting Contract

The Community DAO Reserve (10M REBL, 1%) is locked until July 1, 2026, then vests linearly over 24 months.

Use cases: Strategic partnerships, community grants, exchange listings, and ecosystem incentives.

Vesting Contract

Revenue from ZORA assets (Creator Coins & Content Coin NFTs) is used to buy back and burn $REBL, increasing scarcity and value for holders.

How it works:

  • 80% Buy-Back & Burn: ETH revenue is bridged to Solana to permanently burn $REBL.
  • 15% Community DAO: Funds future initiatives and buy-back campaigns.
  • 5% Operations: Covers essential costs.

Result: Trading activity on ZORA directly boosts $REBL value on Solana through deflationary pressure.

Solana ($REBL): Mint, freeze, and update authorities are renounced. LP tokens locked for 1 year.

ZORA (NFTs): Founder's Creator Coins are vested over 5 years. Smart contracts are non-upgradeable.

Future DAO will use $REBL for voting. Holders of both Creator Coin + Content Coin NFT may get enhanced governance rights.
