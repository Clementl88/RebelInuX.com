THE JOURNEY CONTEST

Community Decides the Next Journey

The Journey Contest determines which historical period and country will be the next Journey NFT collection. 60% Governance Hub vote + 40% social engagement.

How It Works

  • Submit: Create an original image featuring the RebelInuX mascot in a specific country and historical period
  • Vote: Governance Hub members vote (60% weight)
  • Engage: Social engagement on X/Twitter (40% weight)
  • Win: Winner receives public credit + crypto rewards + $rebelinux + free mint of first video NFT
  • Produce: Project creates AI-animated Journey NFTs based on winning theme

Scoring Formula

Final Score = (Vote Score × 0.6) + (Engagement Score × 0.4)

Vote Score (60%): Each Governance Hub member has 1 equal vote. 48-hour voting period. Quorum: 10 members minimum.

Engagement Score (40%): Measured from official contest posts on X/Twitter (Likes, Reposts, Replies).

The Prize

The winning theme becomes the project's next official Journey. Winner receives:

  • Public credit (recognition as contest winner)
  • Crypto rewards (amounts announced per contest)
  • $rebelinux Creator Coins
  • Free mint of the first video NFT from the collection

Reward Loop

The newly minted Journey NFT collection becomes the required set for the following epoch. Holders need the most recent Journey NFT from this collection to be eligible for $REBL rewards.

A new contest begins once the current Journey is completed. Duration varies by historical theme.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the REBL Governance Hub?

Hold at least 500,000 $REBL tokens in your wallet, then use the Collab.Land verification link to connect your wallet (signature only, no private keys). You'll be automatically verified and granted access to the private Telegram group.

Why 1 member = 1 vote instead of token-weighted?

Equal voting prevents whale dominance and ensures every qualified participant has the same say. This creates true community governance where ideas matter more than wallet size.

How does the Journey Contest scoring work?

60% Governance Hub vote + 40% social engagement. Each Governance Hub member has 1 equal vote. Engagement is measured from official contest posts on X/Twitter (Likes, Reposts, Replies).

What happens to the Community Reserve?

The 10M $REBL Community Reserve is locked until July 1, 2026. Upon unlock, control will be transferred to the REBL Governance Hub for community-governed initiatives including grants, partnerships, and ecosystem incentives.

Is Collab.Land verification safe?

Yes! Collab.Land is non-custodial and only requires a wallet signature (not a transaction). No private keys or seed phrases are ever required. It's read-only access used by thousands of reputable Web3 communities.

When will the on-chain DAO launch?

If the community grows to demand a full on-chain DAO, a vote within the Governance Hub will initiate that transition. Until then, the REBL Governance Hub remains the definitive decision-making body.
