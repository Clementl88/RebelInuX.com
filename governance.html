Governance FAQ

How do I join the REBL Governance Hub?

To join the exclusive Telegram Governance Hub, you need to hold at least 500,000 $REBL tokens in your wallet. Then use the Collab.Land verification link to connect your wallet (signature only, no private keys) and gain access to the group.

What's the difference between Governance Hub and the future DAO?

The Governance Hub is for real-time discussions and proposal shaping (active now). The future DAO will handle on-chain voting for treasury management and protocol decisions (planned Q3 2026). They work together - ideas from the Hub can become DAO proposals.

Can I participate without buying NFTs or 500K $REBL?

Yes! While RebelJudges requires Voting Key NFTs and the Governance Hub requires 500K $REBL, the future DAO will use $REBL tokens for voting with no minimum (1 $REBL = 1 vote). Everyone can participate in community discussions regardless of holdings.

What will the DAO control when it launches?

The DAO will control: Treasury management, Partnership approvals, Protocol upgrades, Grant distributions, Exchange listing decisions, and other strategic financial decisions affecting the RebelInuX ecosystem.

When does the DAO treasury vesting begin?

The 10M $REBL DAO treasury begins vesting on July 1, 2026, with linear vesting over 24 months. This gives time for the DAO to be fully operational before treasury funds become available.

Is the Collab.Land verification safe?

Yes! Collab.Land is non-custodial and only requires a wallet signature (not a transaction). No private keys or seed phrases are ever required. It's read-only access to verify your balance and is used by thousands of reputable Web3 communities.
