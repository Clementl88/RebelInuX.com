About Journey Collectibles

Journey Collectibles are AI-generated videos created by the project, retracing important historical events. Each Journey is a collection of multiple video Collectibles. The subject of each Journey is determined by the REBL Governance Hub through the Journey Contest (60% vote + 40% engagement scoring).

French Revolution Journey: Currently 4 chapters minted, with more chapters coming. The community decides when the Journey concludes and the next contest begins.

To earn weekly $REBL rewards: Hold all three assets — $rebelinux Creator Coin + Permanent Rebel Key + the most recent Journey Collectible.

Artwork & NFTs FAQ

What are Journey Collectibles?

Journey Collectibles are AI-generated videos created by the project, retracing important historical events. Each Journey is a collection of multiple video Collectibles. The subject of each Journey is determined by the community through the Journey Contest.

What are the three required assets for rewards?

To qualify for weekly $REBL rewards, you must hold: 1) $rebelinux Creator Coin, 2) Permanent Rebel Key, and 3) the most recent Journey Collectible. Register your Solana address once on the Rebel Key ZORA page.

Where can I buy/sell Journey Collectibles?

All RebelInuX Journey Collectibles are exclusively available on ZORA at zora.co/@rebelinux. Connect your wallet to view, purchase, or trade.

How can I submit my artwork?

Share your artwork on Telegram, Twitter/X, or Discord using the links above. Include your social handle and wallet address for credit and prizes.

What is the Journey Contest?

The Journey Contest is a community voting mechanism where REBL Governance Hub members decide the next Journey theme. It uses hybrid scoring: 60% vote + 40% social engagement. Winners receive public credit, crypto rewards, and free mints.
