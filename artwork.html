About Journey Collectibles

Journey Collectibles are AI-generated videos retracing important historical events. Each Journey is a collection of multiple video Collectibles. The subject of each Journey is determined by the REBL Governance Hub through the Journey Contest (60% vote + 40% engagement scoring).

To earn weekly $REBL rewards: Hold all three assets — $rebelinux Creator Coin + Permanent Rebel Key + the most recent Journey Collectible.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Journey Collectibles?

AI-generated videos retracing important historical events. Each Journey is a collection of multiple video Collectibles. The subject is determined by the community through the Journey Contest.

What are the three required assets for rewards?

To qualify for weekly $REBL rewards, hold: 1) $rebelinux Creator Coin, 2) Permanent Rebel Key, and 3) the most recent Journey Collectible. Register your Solana address once on the Rebel Key ZORA page.

Where can I buy Journey Collectibles?

All RebelInuX Journey Collectibles are exclusively available on ZORA at zora.co/@rebelinux. Connect your wallet to purchase.

What is the Journey Contest?

Community voting mechanism where REBL Governance Hub members decide the next Journey theme. Hybrid scoring: 60% vote + 40% social engagement. Winners receive credit, crypto rewards, and free mints.

How can I submit my artwork?

Share your artwork on Telegram, Twitter/X, or Discord using the links above. Include your social handle and wallet address for credit.
